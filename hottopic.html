Infuse Your Night With Flavour

Mixologist Matt28 - 10 - 2016

Creating wonderfully different flavours isn’t as hard as it sounds! At MBE we follow the basic rules of infusing different alcohols with different flavours; basically we ram a whole load of something into some booze and see what happens!

Matching the flavours to the alcohol can be the trickiest part. If you’d like a delicious Fennel and Tarragon Gin you’d want a gin with few additional botanicals so they don’t collide with your added flavours. Vodka is the best booze to use as it has no true flavour; you can see our Strawberry and Basil Vodka causing quite a stir in our Caipiroskas.

Length of time is also a big deal. Not enough time and our Chilli Vodka gives you a little tickle in your mouth, but too much will blow your head clean off. At MBE we have the delightful job of testing the flavours daily; sight, smell and taste are the best ways, but trial and error plays a large part also.

Pairing infusions to cocktails is the best part. When we make a new infusion it’s always good fun to test them in our Classic cocktails. We have obvious pairs, such as our Coffee Bean Vodka in an Espresso Martini for a double-strong hit of caffeine and flavour, but the more obscure ones, like our Watermelon and Basil Rum for Mojitos, have proved to be incredibly odd but equally delicious!

If you have any questions about any of the infusions mentioned, or how we can produce a unique infusion for your evening, feel free to get in touch.

Know a great flavour combo? We’re always looking for new ideas so let us know on our Facebook and Instagram pages.
