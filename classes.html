Shaken & Stirred

This is MBE’s basic package, allowing participants to engage in some basic cocktail making skills. This 1.5 hour session will start with a sparkling cocktail of the season, followed by a guided demonstration of 2 completely different cocktails; 007 will certainly enjoy one of them! Guests will then mix up their own Cosmopolitan, then using the skills they have been shown will be split into teams to create a bespoke cocktail to win over our tame Mixologist.

This package is perfect for any event; birthdays, hen parties, corporate team- builds, BBQ’s you name it you’ll certainly be leaving Shaken and Stirred!
