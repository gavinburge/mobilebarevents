Who Are We?

Welcome to Mobile Bartending & Events! We offer mobile bartending services, specialising in high quality cocktails!

Planning a Hen Party? Why not try one of our Interactive Cocktail Classes where everyone can show off their bartending skills before hitting the town!

Or maybe a Big Birthday Bash? Work with our Mixologists to create your unique menu, then sit back and watch your very own Bartender bring it to life!

For more information on MBE have a look at our About Us page, otherwise get in touch and let us mix up a great night for you!
